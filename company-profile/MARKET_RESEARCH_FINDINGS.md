## 3. Why Government Dependency is Risky

### Evidence from Research:

1. **VC Funding Crisis** - State-owned VCs (BRI Ventures, MDI Ventures) involved in corruption
2. **Slow Execution** - Roadmaps exist but implementation lags
3. **Regulatory Uncertainty** - Rules change frequently
4. **Political Risk** - Changes in administration affect priorities
5. **Successful Companies Bootstrap** - Amartha (15 years, profitable, $55M funding from international sources, NOT government)

### Quote from Expert:
> "The tech winter in Indonesia and ASEAN is impacted by governance issues, resulting in a shortage of funding for later-stage startups."
> — Rudiantara, Former Minister of Communication and Information

### Amartha Success Story (Counter-example):
- 15 years old fintech
- Disbursed **Rp 35 trillion ($2.1B)** to 3.3 million customers
- Funded by **international sources** (Swedfund, Finnfund, BIO, IFC)
- Focus on **integrity and governance**
- Did NOT rely on government/domestic VCs
