## English Version

### VISION

> **"Transforming industries through smart, connected solutions."**

_(6 words - concise, memorable, no geographic limitation)_

### MISSION

We empower businesses to achieve operational excellence through integrated IoT solutions.

1. **DELIVER** end-to-end solutions that connect hardware, software, and cloud platforms seamlessly

2. **ENABLE** real-time monitoring and data-driven decisions that reduce downtime and costs

3. **BUILD** lasting partnerships with clients across manufacturing, energy, logistics, and maritime sectors

4. **DEVELOP** continuous expertise in IoT, AI, and emerging technologies to stay ahead of industry needs

5. **UPHOLD** the highest standards of integrity and professionalism in everything we do

## Alignment with Research Findings

### Why No "Indonesia" in Vision

Based on market research:

1. **Government Unreliable** - State VCs involved in corruption scandals
2. **Funding Collapsed** - 95% drop, domestic investor confidence gone
3. **Batam = Gateway** - Singapore proximity enables regional expansion
4. **Asia Pacific Fastest Growing** - 26% CAGR, why limit to one country?
5. **Success Stories are Independent** - Amartha funded by international sources

### Why Integrity is Emphasized

Based on market research:

1. **eFishery Scandal** - $1.4B unicorn, revenue inflated 5x
2. **TaniHub Scandal** - $25M fraud involving state VCs
3. **Investor Confidence Eroded** - Governance is now a differentiator
4. **Amartha Success** - 15 years, profitable, built on integrity
